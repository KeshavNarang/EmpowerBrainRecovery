EmpowerBrainRecovery.org

Empowering Young Adults with Brain Injuries Through Support and Rehabilitation

Our mission is to bridge critical gaps in care through peer-led support, rehabilitation services, and community integration.

Our Mission

EmpowerBrainRecovery is a student-led initiative dedicated to transforming the lives of young adults and individuals affected by brain injuries, including Traumatic Brain Injuries (TBI) and Acquired Brain Injuries (ABI). We aim to empower individuals with personalized rehabilitation, academic and career support, and community reintegration. We are committed to delivering holistic, patient-centered care, advocating for equitable policies, and raising awareness about the challenges faced by those with ABI and TBI.

What We Offer

Rehabilitation

Partnerships with leading rehab centers to provide holistic, culturally sensitive care.

Academic Support

Integrating school into recovery to help students build skills in executive functions, learning strategies, and coursework management.

Career Development

Collaboration with organizations like Penn Career Services to offer resume writing, job applications, and skills workshops.

Policy Advocacy

Lobbying for policies to improve vocational rehabilitation and accommodations for individuals with brain injuries.

Community Integration

Creating programs that enhance social and life skills for successful reintegration, in collaboration with local organizations.

Life Skills Development

Workshops on budgeting, independent living, and day-to-day management to empower survivors to build confidence and independence.
